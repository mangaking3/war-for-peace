They would cut up my body and reconnect it to different creatures.” She wiped tears that flowed out of her eyes.

Clara continued, “They sucked my mana and placed it in the king. I was sucked dry and they were about to kill me when Joker came to save me. He then brought me to this school. Ryuu’s anger dissipated from his eyes slightly. He began to feel some empathy for Clara as he once experienced the same thing. Clara wiped her tears and resumed her story, “I trained hard so that no one would judge me. A few months later, there was an announcement that there were potions, called soul potions, that could forcefully insert mythical spirits into your body. They cost at least 50 million geos. I was thinking of how to steal it when I found a stone that would make me invisible. It was a game changer that allowed me to devise a new plan.”
